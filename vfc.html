Our Story

The Tea That Started
With a "Faltu" Idea

It started in the narrow lanes of Bagnan, Howrah — a small stall, a big dream, and a name nobody forgot.

"Why name it 'Faltu'? Because we knew the name would make you stop. And the chai would make you stay."

What began as a humble cha-er dokaan near Bagnan rapidly turned into West Bengal's most talked-about chai brand. Our vision was simple: serve the best Darjeeling tea at prices every student, office-goer, and rickshaw-wala could afford.

Today, VFC — Vagnar Faltu Cha — stands as a symbol of Bengali entrepreneurship, authentic taste, and viral branding that money can't manufacture.

🎯
Our Mission
To make authentic, affordable Darjeeling chai accessible to every corner of India — from Bagnan to Bangalore.
🌟
Our Vision
500+ outlets across India by 2027. Becoming the most recognized chai franchise brand in South Asia.
💡
The Name "Faltu"
In Bengali, "Faltu" means "useless" or "ordinary." We flipped the script — our name is faltu, but our chai is anything but.
🤲
Community First
VFC creates local employment, supports small franchise owners, and brings quality chai to neighborhoods across West Bengal.
